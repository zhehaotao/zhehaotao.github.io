E commerce shop

E commerce shop app is a full-functional web app built with React.

  • User sign in with passport or Google account.
  • Edit cart items in the checkout page.
  • Online payment demo with credit card or Alipay.

    • Share trip

    Share your amazing trip on this website. You can upload photo from your phone, pad or computer and write feelings about this trip. You can also "like" and comment on other people's trip. Come and join our great community to share and make friends.

    Data visualization

    An interactive data visualization tool for excel.

  • Parse the excel and show corresponding plot according to user selected fields. Display a bar plot when two fields are given and a scatter plot for three fields.
  • Plot can be updated based on editable data and user input title.
  • Export edited data in csv and corresponding plot in png and jpg.
